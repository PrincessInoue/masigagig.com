COLD CHAIN

Consistent adherence to temperature range requirement throughout the supply chain (transport, handling, packaging, and storage.)


Temperature backing - temperature tracking across the chain to confirm
appropriate temperature throughout.


Proper Handling and Packaging - use of proper containers
that stabilizes temperature.


Storage - temeperature controlled warehouse environment
and/or storage equipment.


Transportation - temeperature controlled warehouse environment vehicles


Distribution Route - planning of transport routes and time of delivery to minimize
temperature variation or higher average temperatures. A more stable external
temperature influences the internal stability.
